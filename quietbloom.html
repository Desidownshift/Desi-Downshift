A Quiet Bloom



In moonlight's hush, the petals gleam,
Soft shadows caught in half a dream,
The jasmine stirs with scented breath,
A whisper in the arms of death.

No sun to guide, no bees in flight,
Yet flowers bloom in silver light.
The stars lean close, the garden sighs—
A quiet world with open eyes.

-Harry
