Outlap Diaries


Outlap Diaries - Stories from the slow sips and sharp turns of life. Not everything in my life runs at 13000 RPM. Some days, it's just me, a piping hot chai, and the leftover adrenaline from a poorly-handled life turn. Outlap Diaries is where I park the tools, switch off the CAD, and open up about the experiences that may or may not fit in a gearbox. This is my personal track diary — full of: Unexpected curves (the emotional kind),
Traffic jams of this journey,
Oversteered friendships,
And those rare, silent straights where everything just makes sense… for 3.2 seconds. Here, I'll share: The awkward, The accidental, The "bro you won't believe what just happened" moments, And the beautiful messes that never make it to LinkedIn — but always deserve a page. Because life, much like a chicane, isn't about speed. It's about how gracefully (or not) you navigate the chaos, preferably with a hot chai in one hand and zero regrets in the rear-view.
