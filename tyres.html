Compound Confusion? Let's Clear It.

Pirelli ‚Äî the official tyre supplier ‚Äî makes five main slick compounds for the season: C1 to C5. The lower the number, the harder the tyre. But they don't just roll up with all five to every track. Instead, they pick a set of three and rename them for simplicity: Hard (white), Medium (yellow), and Soft (red).

Hard tyres last long but grip like a disinterested intern. Soft tyres? Sticky as hell, but wear out quicker than free Wi-Fi at a railway station. Mediums? The goldilocks zone. The trick is finding the right compound for the right circuit ‚Äî and then praying the weather doesn't throw a tantrum.

Tracks like Monza love hard rubber. Monaco? Go soft or go home. It's chess on asphalt, with Pirelli playing supply god.
