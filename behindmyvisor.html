Behind my Visor!


I'm Hrishikesh H L (Harry).
Mechanical engineering student by degree, race junkie by choice, and currently trying to convince my laptop to not crash mid-simulation at 3AM.

This site? It's not a portfolio. It's a pitstop for all the chaos in my head — greasy thoughts, chai-fueled rants, and a dream that smells like burnt clutch and MIG welds. If you're here for clean, curated engineering, Bud, wrong garage.

I didn't grow up building cars or fabricating masterpieces — I just watched one too many "how to machine a knuckle" & "Rust to Riches" episodes and now I think I'm built for the pits & the Manufacturing units. I haven't snapped a bolt or set anything on fire yet, but I'm emotionally ready. I crave the late nights, the grinder burns, the existential crisis over a misaligned bracket. Right now I mostly lurk, learn, and pretend to understand datasheets — but give it time. I'm already in too deep, and I haven't even ruined my first tap set. Today, I'm neck-deep in simulations, FEA errors, and that one CAD file that won't open. But the more I learn, the deeper I spiral into this whole beautiful mess of motorsport manufacturing.

This isn't just about engineering. Or racing.
It's about me — my breakdowns, breakthroughs, metal-shaving mayhem, poetry, playlists and confessions I've chosen to make public. Sometimes it's code. Sometimes it's chaos. Sometimes it's both.

Why "Desi Downshift"?
Because every time I hit the brakes, heel-toe like a maniac, and shift down, I remember where I started—from desi streets of Coimbatore to racing dreams. This isn't just engineering. It's attitude, grit, and desi-engineered chaos control.
So, welcome to Desi Downshift — torque down your doubts, and take the ride!
